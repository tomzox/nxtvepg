nexTView
EPG Decoder Software

Welcome to the homepage of the nxtvepg software package: A decoder for nexTView EPG, an Electronic TV Programme Guide for the analog domain (as opposed to the various digital EPGs that come with most digital broadcasts, although you can receive Nextview EPG digitally, too). It enables you to receive and browse free TV programme schedules for all of the major networks in Germany, Austria, Switzerland, France and Belgium with an analog TV card, without access to the internet. New release 2.7.0 (April/2/2004)

Main focus of the new release 2.7.0 was maintenance, hence there's not much big news, but lots of bug fixes and minor improvements.

  • The only major new feature is a change in shortcut presentation from the previous flat list into a hierarchical tree, i.e. now you can organize your shortcuts in nested folders and separator lines (see screenshot)
  • Improved VBI (teletext signal) decoder for weak or distorted antenna signals to reduce data loss due to decoding errors during acquisition.
  • Added work-arounds for problems with channel identification on French Arte and France5.
  • Extended command line interface for the -daemon and dump modes, i.e. for users who use nxtvepg mainly for background data acquisition and want to control it by scripts or batch files.
  • Improved support for the latest generation of video4linux drivers (v4l2), esp. to avoid interruptions during recording or TV viewing by channel changes for Nextview EPG acquisition.
  • Windows only: extended support for new TV cards and tuners (e.g. tuners MT2050 and MK3) and added work-around for conflicts with the card vendors' drivers for CX23881 based cards (as always, thanks to the DScaler team)
  • ...plus too many fixes to list them all here.

Get the new release from the download section. For a complete list, including more details and a list of bug fixes and improvements see the changelog.

Questions and feedback

Your feedback is always welcome. If you have specific questions, suggestions, bug reports or any other kind of feedback, feel free to use any of the following possibilities:

  • Read the list of Frequently Asked Questions (including answers)
  • Discussion forum: Preferred place for questions, feature requests, bug reports or exchange of tips & tricks between users (does not require Sourceforge registration).
  • Mailing list: Currently used only for announcements of pre-releases. But it's also open for technical discussions between developer(s) and users of the nxtvepg software. Requires subscription.
  • If you only want to be kept up to date about official releases, subscribe to the nxtvepg project on freshmeat.net.

Before posting a question, please check if it isn't answered in the manual and look through the mailing list archives and the forum if the same question hasn't been asked recently.

Help wanted

If you're a software developer and like nxtvepg but want to help to make it even more useful, or if you're just generally seeking a freeware project to work on, please consider contributing to nxtvepg. I've compiled a list of topics which would benefit from your help the most. Note that you can also contribute by adding nxtvepg support to other applications, e.g. by adding Nextview EPG on-screen-display to a TV viewing application.
