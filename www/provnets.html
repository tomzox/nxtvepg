Notes about TRT: The same service was provided via Eurasiasat on the digitally transmitted networks FashionTV, NumberOne TV and Discovery Channel TV.

This service started in May 2001. It took them a while to get all the network ID codes right, but as of June 2002 only the CNI for NBC was incorrect anymore (CNI values 5 instead of 0x8E71). One blatant error in this service was that all networks that transmitted it identified themselves as TRT-1 both via packet 8/30/1 and Nextview application inventory, so that one got wrong programme information for those networks when interacting with a TV application.

Notes about TV5: Stopped service on Nov/18/2001, but re-surfaced in November 2002. In March 2005 the network list was extended from only 7 (TV5, TF1, F2, F3, M6, TSR1, TSR2) to 29. At the same time the preview was extended from 4 to up to 8 days (for selected networks only.)

Notes about M6: Stopped service after December/31/2002. Re-surfaced end of March 2003, however reduced from 7 days preview to 3 days only.
