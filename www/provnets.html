Notes about TRT:

This service started in May 2001. As of August they unfortunately still are not sending the correct country/network codes for non-Turkish networks (note: CNI codes are published by the ETSI for all European TV networks in technical report 101 231). Instead, they're simply numbered through starting with 1. When you merge the TRT database with other providers, some networks may appear twice, because they appear to be different by their CNI codes.

Another error is that TRT-int (from which I receive the TRT Nextview) is identifying itself as TRT-1 both via packet 8/30/1 and Nextview application inventory, so that you'll get wrong programme information for this network.

Besides that, not all Turkish (Latin-5) characters can be displayed by nxtvepg. The difference to latin-1 is only 6 characters ('g' and 'G' with half circle above, 'I' with dot above, 'i' without dot, 's' and 'S' with comma beneath). Currently there are no plans to support latin-5 in nxtvepg because that would require mixed use of fonts. Non-supported characters are replaced by their ASCII counterparts.
