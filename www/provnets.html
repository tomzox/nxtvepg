News: As of May 2001 there's a new provider: TRT, which covers mainly Turkish networks plus some international networks like CNN and NBC. However it seems they're still in a testing phase (see note below).

Notes about TRT: As of May/25/2001 this Nextview service appears to be still in a testing phase. Currently they are not using the correct country and network identification codes (CNI) yet. Instead, they're using numbers #1-#23. It will not be possible to merge EPG info from TRT with other providers (note: CNI codes are published by the ETSI for all European TV networks in technical report 101 231)

Update June/1/2001: they are now using the correct CNI codes at least for Turkish networks. Except that TRT-int (from which I receive the TRT Nextview) is identifying itself as TRT-1 both via packet 8/30/1 and Nextview AI, so that you'll get wrong programme information for this network.

More importantly, not all Turkish (Latin-5) characters can be displayed. The difference to latin-1 is only 6 characters ('g' and 'G' with half circle above, 'I' with dot above, 'i' without dot, 's' and 'S' with comma beneath). Currently there are no plans to support latin-5 in nxtvepg because that would require mixed use of fonts. Non-supported characters are replaced by their ASCII counterparts.
