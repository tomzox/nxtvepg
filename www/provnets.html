Notes about TRT:

The same service is provided via Eurasiasat on the digitally transmitted networks FashionTV, NumberOne TV and Discovery Channel TV.

This service started in May 2001. It took them a while to get all the network ID codes right, but as of June 2002 only the CNI for NBC is incorrect anymore (it's 5 instead of 0x8E71). One blatant error in this service is that all networks that transmit it are identifying themselves as TRT-1 both via packet 8/30/1 and Nextview application inventory, so that you'll get wrong programme information for those networks when interacting with a TV application.

Besides that, not all Turkish (Latin-5) characters can be displayed by nxtvepg. The difference to latin-1 is only 6 characters ('g' and 'G' with half circle above, 'I' with dot above, 'i' without dot, 's' and 'S' with comma beneath). Currently there are no plans to support latin-5 in nxtvepg because that would require mixed use of fonts. Non-supported characters are replaced by their ASCII counterparts.

Notes about TV5:

Stopped service on Nov/18/2001, but re-surfaced in November 2002.

March 2005 the network list was extended from only 7 (TV5, TF1, F2, F3, M6, TSR1, TSR2) to the current 29. At the same time the preview was extended from 4 to up to 8 days (for selected networks only.)

Notes about M6:

Stopped service after December/31/2002. Re-surfaced end of March 2003, however reduced from 7 days preview to 3 days only.
